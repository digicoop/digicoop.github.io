Digicoop develops online collaborative tools to help team work better together so that they can deliver their projects or reach their goals more efficiently with a more collaborative approach.

We are a French team that is passionate about technology, the web and what it can bring to society. We are using our extensive experience to build great products that we hope have a positive impact.

We believe that businesses need to bring back some of their focus to the people which they are made of. That's why we have chosen to create Digicoop as a worker cooperative, meaning a company owned by its employees (known as SCOP in France). On top of that we are remote-first, almost completely paperless and managed using Kantree.

You can contact us at contact@digicoop.io.
